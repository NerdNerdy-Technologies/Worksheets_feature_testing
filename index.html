What is JumpHats?

JumpHats is a brand committed to creating technology tools to empower parents, teachers and other stakeholders in providing individualised and multidisciplinary early learning for their child.

The vision behind JumpHats

We understand that the one-size-fits-all model of education is outdated and needs a revamp. Thus, we provide the personalised solution: (in line with the targets of the NEP set out by the Govt. of India).

Where do we come in?

This browser worksheet tool enables you to create unique worksheets with interdisciplinary concepts, that can be customised to the child’s learning pace. JumpHats helps you explore your child’s interests and build upon their strengths

Who is behind it?

Built by Chahat Vasal and Akshat Tulsani in association with NerdNerdy Technologies. Don’t let your children’s learning be confined to the classroom books, feed their curiosity and help them grow to their full potential!
